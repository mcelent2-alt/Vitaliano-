  • • Mooers’ diary documents meetings with von Neumann, the earliest on August 29, 1945 — learns about ENIAC and plans for EDVAC
  • • October 28, 1945: Mooers and NOL team visit ENIAC in Philadelphia, then attend a meeting at the IAS where von Neumann lectured on his tentative coding scheme
  • • Mooers’ notebook includes a citation for Turing’s paper; he requests a photostat copy of it from the library
  • • March 1946: Mooers tracks down the work of McCulloch and Pitts and uses neuron notation/cybernetic terminology to sketch plans for a “thinking machine”
  • • Summer 1946: Mooers discusses his plans with Pitts; his boss John V. Atanasoff orders him to work on more useful matters
